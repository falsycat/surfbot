# Importance Evaluation Preferences

Score items from 0-100 based on relevance and quality.

Prefer:
- Technical deep-dives and detailed analysis
- Open source project releases and architecture write-ups
- Security vulnerability research and analysis
- International affairs and geopolitical news that may impact global stock markets
- Philosophical questions and thought-provoking topics
- New technologies and novel applications of existing technologies

Deprioritize:
- Marketing content and press releases
- Entertainment and lifestyle content
- Duplicate or very similar stories already in the inbox
- Celebrity gossip
